About Us

Our Website

The First Step is an E-Book Website which provides E-Books for the college students. We have started this for the 1st and 2nd year students of four renowed colleges namely SGSITS , IET - DAVV , Medicaps Indore and BIT, Durg. Not only this, we provide you with some fascinating novels. As you know, Engineers are book lovers. Moreover, novels are considered a good source of entertainment and we've tried to put all the novels of the best sellers on this website.

Our Mission

The First Step is started as an alternative to books. Our website contains many E-Books which will surely save you time that you waste while finding those on the internet. Currently we have E-books for 1st year only but definitely, in the coming months our mission is to provide the E-Books for all the year. Moreover, we will not limit ourselves to two colleges only. As the time progresses, our motto is to cover all the colleges of Indore.
